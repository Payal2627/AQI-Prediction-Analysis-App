What is AQI?

Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized measure used to indicate how clean or polluted the air is and what associated health effects might concern individuals. This Air Quality Index Analysis and Prediction system uses Machine Learning techniques to estimate AQI values from pollutant concentrations and provide meaningful environmental insights.
